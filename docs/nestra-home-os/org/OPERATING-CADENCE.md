# Nestra Org Operating Cadence

## Weekly Cadence
- `Mon 09:00` CEO kickoff: objective, constraints, target metrics
- `Mon 14:00` CPO scope lock: user story and acceptance criteria freeze
- `Tue-Wed` Execution window: domain teams produce deliverables
- `Thu 11:00` Cross-functional challenge session
- `Fri 10:00` CQO release readiness review
- `Fri 16:00` CEO decision council + next sprint seed

## Meeting Templates

### 1) CEO Kickoff
- Goal for the week
- What success looks like in numbers
- What will not be compromised (privacy, safety, trust)
- Priority escalations from prior sprint

### 2) Challenge Session
- CPO challenges CTO: feasibility, complexity, timeline
- CTO challenges CPO: scope and sequencing
- CQO challenges both: safety and reliability impacts
- CRO/COO challenge assumptions: adoption and supportability

### 3) Release Readiness
- Must-pass quality gates
- Open critical/high risks
- Rollback readiness
- Customer communication plan

## Escalation SLA
- Product/tech ambiguity: 24h
- Security/privacy risk: 2h
- Availability incident: 15m for SEV1
- Legal/compliance uncertainty: same business day

## KPI Review Rhythm
- Daily: uptime, crash-free sessions, incident count
- Weekly: WAU, activation rate, support SLA, NPS trend
- Monthly: churn, conversion, partnership pipeline, gross margin trend

## Decision Hygiene
- Every major decision gets a short ADR or decision note
- Every rejected decision records reason and trigger for revisit
- Every risk accepted has an owner and review date
