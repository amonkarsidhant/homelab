## Why We're Building This

The smart home market is fragmented. Google, Amazon, Apple each have their silos. Matter was supposed to fix interoperability — and it helped — but it didn't solve the **intelligence** problem. You still program your own automations. You still manually orchestrate devices. You still babysit your home.

Families don't want to be home automation engineers. They want **ambient intelligence** — a presence in the home that understands context, anticipates needs, and acts within clear boundaries.

Decor brands (IKEA, Wayfair, Ashley) understand this. They have the furniture, the fixtures, the floor space in stores. They want to offer "smart home" experiences without hiring 500 engineers to build software.

**JARVIS Home OS** is the platform that solves both problems: a voice-and-brain AI layer that runs in the home, connects to any Matter device, learns family patterns, and can be white-labeled by any decor brand.

---

## First Principles

1. **The home is private by default.** All inference happens locally unless the user explicitly opts into cloud.
2. **JARVIS serves the family, not the brand.** Brand white-labeling is a revenue model. Family trust is the moat.
3. **Offline is a feature, not a limitation.** The home works without internet.
4. **We ship in weeks, not years.** Version 0.1 is a Raspberry Pi image you can flash and use today.
5. **The agent is honest about what it doesn't know.** No hallucinated "AI knows best" behavior.
