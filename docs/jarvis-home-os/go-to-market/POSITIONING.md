## The One-Line Pitch

> **"JARVIS is a privacy-first home brain for people who want a smart home that just works — without being an automation engineer."**

---

## The Sentence That Goes With It

Most "smart homes" require you to program them. JARVIS learns your home instead — your routines, your preferences, the way your family actually lives — and handles the small stuff so you don't have to think about it. It runs locally, works offline, and belongs to you, not to Google or Amazon.

---

## Three Audience Versions

### For the Non-Technical Homeowner

"You know how your phone learns your habits? JARVIS does that for your whole house. It notices when you're usually home, which lights you use most, when you like the thermostat set. Then it quietly handles things for you — turning off lights you forgot, warming the house before you wake up — without you ever opening an app or writing a single rule. And if you don't want any of that, it just stays out of your way. Everything stays on your own hardware, in your own home. No big tech company listening."

### For the Power User / Home Automation Enthusiast

"JARVIS is a local-first Matter hub with a built-in voice agent and pattern learning engine. Think of it as Home Assistant's more approachable sibling — you get the flexibility to automate anything, but JARVIS's contextual reasoning means you spend less time writing YAML and more time with a home that actually anticipates what you want. It's self-hosted, offline-capable, and built on open standards (Matter, local STT). If you've ever wished your smart home just *got* you without 40 hours of configuration, JARVIS is built for that."

### For the Brand Executive (Decor / Furniture)

"JARVIS is a white-label smart home brain your engineering team can embed in weeks, not years. You have the furniture, the floor space, the customer relationships. You don't have — and shouldn't need to build — a 200-person software division. JARVIS handles the intelligence layer: local voice control, pattern learning, Matter device management. Your brand gets its own smart home experience — your voice, your UX, your app — while we maintain the platform. Homeowners get a seamless experience; you get a new revenue stream and a differentiated in-store story."

## Competitor Differentiation

### vs. Home Assistant

| Dimension | Home Assistant | JARVIS |
|-----------|----------------|--------|
| Target User | Automators, developers | Non-technical homeowners |
| Setup Experience | YAML config, manual | Flash and go (target) |
| Voice | Add-on, manual | Built-in, local |
| Brand White-Label | No | Yes (Enterprise tier) |
| Pattern Learning | Rule-based automation | Proactive suggestions |
| Privacy | Local, strong | Local + family-first framing |
| Community | Large, enthusiast | Growing, mainstream target |

Home Assistant is the 10-year overnight success. JARVIS doesn't compete with HA for its users — we compete for the 95% of homeowners who tried HA and bounced off the YAML.

### vs. Google Home

| Dimension | Google Home | JARVIS |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| Data Privacy | Cloud-first, Google mines data | Local-first, opt-in cloud |
| Offline Mode | No | Yes |
| Brand White-Label | No | Yes |
| Learning | Device usage only | Full-home context |
| Ecosystem Lock-in | High (Google ecosystem) | None (Matter-native) |
| Monthly Cost | $6.99/month (Nest Aware) | Free or $9.99 Home+ |

Google Home is the default, but homeowners are increasingly aware they're paying with their data. JARVIS offers a credible alternative with zero lock-in.

### vs. Amazon Alexa

| Dimension | Amazon Alexa | JARVIS |
|-----------|--------------|--------|
| Local Processing | Cloud-only | Local by default |
| Privacy | Amazon records everything | Nothing leaves home without opt-in |
| Matter Support | Partial | Full Matter bridge |
| Smart Home Routines | Device-focused | Contextual, whole-home |
| Brand White-Label | No | Yes |
| Skill Ecosystem | Large but fragmented | Growing, curated |

Amazon's dominance in voice is real, but their smart home play is hardware-first (Echo) and their privacy track record is poor. JARVIS wins on trust.

### vs. Samsung SmartThings

| Dimension | SmartThings | JARVIS |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| Hub Architecture | Cloud-dependent | Local-first |
| Matter Support | Late and partial | Matter-native |
| Automation Engine | Scene-based | Pattern learning + agentic |
| Brand White-Label | Enterprise-only | Accessible OEM tier |
| Developer Model | Proprietary | Developer tier with open API |
| Privacy | Samsung data practices | Local + family-first |

Samsung's strength is hardware. Their software story is fragmented and cloud-heavy. JARVIS fills the gap for brands that want a smart home story without Samsung's complexity.

## Positioning Statement Framework

**For homeowners who want smart home convenience without the complexity and privacy trade-offs of big tech, JARVIS is a local-first home intelligence platform that learns family patterns and handles home automation automatically, unlike Home Assistant's YAML config, Google Home's cloud dependency, or Amazon Alexa's data mining.**

---

## Key Messaging Pillars

1. **Privacy You Can Verify** — All inference runs locally. Nothing leaves your home without explicit opt-in.
2. **Home That Learns** — JARVIS notices patterns and suggests automations you didn't know you wanted.
3. **Works Without the Internet** — Cloud goes down, your home keeps running.
4. **No Ecosystem Lock-in** — Matter-native. Any certified device works.
5. **Your Home, Your Brand** — Decor brands can white-label JARVIS with their own identity.
