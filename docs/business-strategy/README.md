## Key Takeaway
**Don't pick based on "cool idea." Pick where your unfair advantage compounds.**

Your unfair advantage is the intersection of:
- Engineering leadership
- Transformation experience
- QA + Chaos Engineering
- Early Agentic AI understanding

**That screams B2B engineering product, not consumer stuff.**

## Ideas Evaluated

### Weak Fit (⚠️)
| Idea | Issue |
|------|-------|
| Dropshipping | Saturated, low margins, no moat, requires marketing skills |
| Inspirational Videos | Weak differentiation, hard to monetize |
| Trading (crypto/stocks) | Speculation, not a business |

### Medium Fit (★)
| Idea | Note |
|------|------|
| AI Automation Agency | Fast revenue, service-based, good starter |
| Niche B2B SaaS | Less competition, but lacks domain expertise initially |

### Strong Fit (★★)
| Idea | Note |
|------|------|
| Transformation Consultancy | Cash engine €10k-100k, time-based, aligns with experience |
| Content Brand (LinkedIn + YouTube) | Support strategy, builds authority long-term |
| App Factory with Agentic AI | Strong fit if niche-focused, meta-product |

### Top Tier (★★★)
| Idea | Note |
|------|------|
| Agentic QA / Dev Productivity SaaS | Perfect fit for combined skills, B2B pricing |
| AI for Engineering Teams Platform | Massive demand, start narrow (QA automation agent) |
| Internal Developer Tools SaaS | Release risk scoring, incident prediction, clear ROI |

## Recommended Strategic Stack

### Phase 1 — 0 to 3 months
- Start **Transformation / AI Consultancy** → immediate cash
- Build **LinkedIn + YouTube Shorts** presence
- Publish chaos engineering experiments publicly
- Goal: Generate revenue + build audience

### Phase 2 — 2 to 6 months
- Identify ONE painful problem in: QA / Dev Productivity / Chaos Engineering
- Start **Agentic QA SaaS** or **Dev Tools product**
- Validate with 3-5 paying pilot customers
- Goal: Product-market fit

### Phase 3 — 6 to 18 months
- Scale SaaS via audience + network effects
- Hire small team (1-2 engineers)
- Move toward product-led growth
- Goal: €5k-20k MRR
