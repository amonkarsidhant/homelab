About Me

Shifting quality left and embedding resilience by design

As Engineering Manager of Resilience and Quality, I champion blameless postmortems, proactive testing, and a culture of continuous improvement. Whether stress-testing architecture or refining recovery playbooks, I focus on engineering for reality—not just theory.

I believe in learning from failures, not fearing them. My approach combines explicit risk management with building quality into teams—instilling practices that catch issues before they reach production.

Beyond Worldline, I'm an active voice in the SRE and Quality communities, sharing lessons from 15+ years in infrastructure, identity, and cloud. I'm fluent in Microsoft technologies (Azure AD, ADFS, Active Directory) and continuously expanding into modern observability and reliability tooling.
